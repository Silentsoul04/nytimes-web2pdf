# Killing the (Facebook) Messenger

<div class="css-53u6y8">

**Q.** *How do I delete Facebook Messenger from my phone?*

**A.** [Facebook Messenger](https://www.messenger.com/features), the
company’s chat program, was spun out of the Facebook mobile app
[in 2011](https://www.facebookcorewwwi.onion/notes/facebook-engineering/building-facebook-messenger/10150259350998920/),
before the social network removed the chat function from the main app
[in 2014](https://bits.blogs.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2014/04/15/facebook-forces-users-to-install-separate-messaging-app/).
As with other third-party apps, you can remove it from your phone with a
few taps.

If you use Messenger on a phone [running a recent version of
Android,](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2521768?hl=en)
open the Settings icon and select Apps & Notifications. Find the
Messenger icon; if you do not see it, tap See All Apps (or App Info)
first. Tap the Uninstall option. You can also delete an app from the
Android home screen by pressing its icon and then dragging it to the
Uninstall symbol that appears at the top of the screen.

</div>

<div class="css-1fanzo5 StoryBodyCompanionColumn">

<div class="css-53u6y8">

You can also remove Android apps by opening the Google Play store icon
on your phone, tapping the three-lined menu icon in the top-left corner
and selecting My Apps & Games. On the next screen, tap the Installed
tab, find the Messenger icon and tap the Open button. When the app’s
page is open, tap the Uninstall button.

</div>

</div>

<div class="css-1fanzo5 StoryBodyCompanionColumn">

<div class="css-53u6y8">

To delete Messenger [on an
iPhone](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT207618), go to the home
screen, find the app’s icon and press your finger on it until the tile
begins to wiggle. Once the wiggling starts, a small X appears in the
icon’s top-left corner. Tap the X, and when the alert box pops up,
confirm you wish to delete the app.

Even if you remove the mobile Messenger app (and do not [delete your
Facebook](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2018/03/21/technology/personaltech/delete-facebook.html)
account), your Facebook friends can still send you messages when you are
[logged into the desktop
version](https://www.facebookcorewwwi.onion/help/735006619902401) of the
site. A stand-alone web version is also available at
[Messenger.com](https://www.messenger.com). If you prefer not to use the
chat function, tell your friends to reach you by other means.
