# John Branch

</div>

## <span></span>

John Branch joined The New York Times in 2005 as a sports reporter. He
won the Pulitzer Prize for feature writing in 2013 for [“Snow
Fall,”](http://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/projects/2012/snow-fall/index.html)
a story about a deadly avalanche in Washington State, and was a finalist
for the prize in 2012 for his [series of
stories](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2011/12/04/sports/hockey/derek-boogaard-a-boy-learns-to-brawl.html)
about Derek Boogaard, a professional hockey player who overdosed on
painkillers. Raised in Colorado, he lives with his family near San
Francisco.
