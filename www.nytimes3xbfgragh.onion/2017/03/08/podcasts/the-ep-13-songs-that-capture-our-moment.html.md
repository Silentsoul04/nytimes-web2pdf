# ‘The EP’: 13 Songs That Capture Our Moment

What songs tell us where music is going? You can read 25 answers to that
question in the [second annual Music Issue from The New York Times
Magazine](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/interactive/2017/03/09/magazine/25-songs-that-tell-us-where-music-is-going.html).

Or you can listen — and hear some of our favorite writers talk about the
songs and the stories that stuck with them this year. “The EP” weaves
together conversation, commentary and music in one multitrack
collection. It’s a window on how we listen to music — and how we talk to
one another about it — right
now.

</div>

</div>

<div style="max-width:100%;margin:0 auto">

<div class="css-17dprlf" data-id="100000004976506" data-slug="the-EP-trailer" style="max-width:600px">

</div>

</div>

<div class="css-1fanzo5 StoryBodyCompanionColumn">

<div class="css-53u6y8">

Some songs are the hits that seem to shout something important about our
culture. Others wormed their ways into writers’ personal lives. And some
stand out because they come from artists we just can’t help trying to
figure out a little better.

</div>

</div>

<div class="css-1fanzo5 StoryBodyCompanionColumn">

<div class="css-53u6y8">

Along the way, you’ll hear about everything from the year’s hottest
vegan rap verse to the band that currently qualifies as the J.J. Abrams
of heavy metal.

You can find and subscribe to “The EP” wherever you listen to podcasts.

## How do I listen?

*If you don’t see an audio player on this page or to subscribe to “The
EP” for free, follow the instructions below.*

**On your iPhone or iPad:**

Open the preloaded app called “Podcasts”; it has a purple icon. If
you’re reading this from your phone, tap [this
link](https://geo.itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-ep-13-songs-that-capture-our-moment/id1213030309?mt=2),
which will take you straight there.

Or if you prefer another podcast player, you can find “The EP” there.
(Here’s [the RSS feed](http://feeds.podtrac.com/tv8uQCHfz8e2).)

**On your Android phone or tablet:**

You can listen and subscribe using the free app Stitcher. If you’re
reading this from your phone, tap [this
link](http://www.stitcher.com/podcast/the-new-york-times/the-ep-13-songs-that-capture-our-moment?refid=stpr).

Or if you prefer another podcast player, you can find “The EP” there.
(Here’s [the RSS feed](http://feeds.podtrac.com/tv8uQCHfz8e2).)

**From a desktop or laptop:**

Scroll up and click the “play” button in the audio player above. Make
sure to keep that window open on your browser if you’re doing other
things, or else the audio will stop.
