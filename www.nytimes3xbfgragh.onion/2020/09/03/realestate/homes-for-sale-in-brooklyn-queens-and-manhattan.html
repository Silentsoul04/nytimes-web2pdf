Homes for Sale in Brooklyn, Queens and Manhattan

This week’s properties are in Bedford-Stuyvesant, Woodside and Kips Bay.

On the Market in New York City

19 Photos

View Slide Show ›

Katherine Marks for The New York Times

By Stefanos Chen

  • Sept. 3, 2020

Click on the slide show to see this week’s featured properties:

  • In Bedford-Stuyvesant: a two-bedroom, two-and-a-half-bath, 1,337-square-foot triplex apartment with a double-height living room and open kitchen on the first level, a mezzanine currently being used as a den, and bedrooms on the top, with a private roof deck in a three-unit building built in 2015.

  • In Woodside: a 1,000-square-foot, two-bedroom, one-bath renovated apartment with a sunny living and dining area on the elevated first floor of a three-story, postwar, walk-up building near the subway.

  • In Kips Bay: a 625-square-foot studio with one bathroom and a sleeping alcove, on the 12th floor of a 1960s co-op with a doorman, laundry, a roof deck, a bike room and parking for a fee.
