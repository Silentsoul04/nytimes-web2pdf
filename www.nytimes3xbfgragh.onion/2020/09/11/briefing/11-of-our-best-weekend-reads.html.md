</div>

Welcome to the weekend. As we start to get into fall, you may feel like
the pandemic and the nonstop news cycle have killed the concept of the
weekend for you. [You’re not
alone](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2020/09/09/style/what-should-i-do-this-weekend.html):
“It feels like perpetual Thursday,” one woman said. If it does seem like
you’re living in the movie “Groundhog Day,” there are still ways [to
enjoy the time off you do
have](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2020/09/10/arts/things-to-do-weekend-coronavirus.html),
even if it’s just by making [this cheesy
toast.](https://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2020/09/09/dining/spinach-rarebit-recipe.html)
Whatever you do, be sure to make time for some great
journalism.
