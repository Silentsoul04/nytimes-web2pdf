# Charlie Savage

</div>

## <span></span>

The Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist Charlie Savage is a Washington
correspondent for The New York Times. He is also the author of “[Power
Wars](http://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2015/12/20/books/review/charlie-savages-power-wars.html),”
published in 2015, an investigative history of national-security legal
policymaking in the Obama administration, and
“[Takeover](http://www.nytimes3xbfgragh.onion/2007/09/25/books/25kaku.html),”
published in 2007, which chronicles the Bush-Cheney administration’s
efforts to expand presidential power.
