Derek Watkins

Derek Watkins is a graphics editor at The New York Times. He uses visuals to cover a range of topics, with a focus on how interactions between people and their environment shape the world. He has a master's degree in geography from the University of Oregon, and previously worked at Columbia University's Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation.
