Rhode Island Election Results

Rhode Island is one of the most solidly Democratic states in the country, and that looks unlikely to change on Election Day.

Gov. Gina Raimondo is favored to win her rematch with Allan Fung, the Republican she defeated in 2014. The state’s two representatives in the House, David Cicilline and Jim Langevin, have both raised vastly more money than their Republican challengers, Patrick Donovan and Salvatore Caiozzo, and are heavy favorites to win re-election.
